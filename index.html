About Me

Hey it's me Goku! Just Kidding, it's Froyo. I started making music ever since my freshman year of high school. I like to make chiptune, orchestal, and videogame soundtrack music. I love anime like Yu yu Hakusho and Hunter X hunter, along with games like Smash bros. Overwatch, and Rivals of Aether. I'm learning Japanese to move and live overseas one day. I analyze data by day and produce by night. Please enjoy my music and buy that shit if you like it and wanna support me.

What I Use [hover/click for more info]

The Linnstrument (200 pads)

Still Used

Primary Midi Keyboard I utilize. Rather expensive but I was able to use the discount provided for college students at the time of purchase

The Vochlea Dubler

Still Used

Secondary Midi device I utilize. Great for prototyping melodies with my voice into FL. Bought the original 1st version on a black Friday for a good discount.

The Korg Nanokey Studio

Not Used Anymore

Midi keyboard I used to use for quite a bit. Very versatile,definitely a solid choice I would recommend for producers of any level.

The Akai Fire

Not Used Anymore

Old Midi Keyboard(s) I had. Good for the beatmaking type, but I seldom use it much anymore. Looks cool when you get 4 of them and hook them all up together. only have 2 of them now

Fl studio (daw of choice)

Will Always Use

My favorite DAW that allows me to flexibly work with a powerful and effective workflow. Much better than Ableton IMO

These're the tools I use mainly for my music. Although certainly not required, they undoubtedly help transferring ideas into tracks. Beware of Gear-acquistion-syndrome
